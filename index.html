What is it like to be a US Alumni?

Being an Alumni from the US Embassy has given me the chance to create a strong network of professionals and to become involved in volunteering for social, innovational and educational projects, working for the Fulbright Alumni Association of Paraguay. It has helped me grow as a better person and it has encouraged me to become a leader, to be a more creative and proactive person, and to believe that the possibilities are endless.

In 2018, I was granted with the Fulbright Scholarship. That made me understand that passion, strength and commitment help dreams come true. Fulbright is synonymous of opportunity, compromise, and empathy. Today, thanks to the Fulbright Scholarship, I have the chance to pursue my higher education in the field of Computer Science at the University of Missouri.
